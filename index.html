Our Story

The Nook is deeply personal an extension of its founder, Shruti Yugal, and her simple belief: if she can’t cook it herself, it doesn’t belong on the menu. Every dish is built from scratch, practiced, refined, and tested with real people before it’s served. There are no shortcuts, no outsourcing just honest, thoughtful food made entirely in-house. What truly sets The Nook apart is this hands-on approach, where the menu is personally crafted, and the experience feels real. Born out of a genuine story rather than a trend, it’s a space that adapts to you whether you come to work, unwind, connect, or simply be and one that people don’t just visit, but keep coming back to.

Shruti Yugal
